    Device Monitoring

    Z-Wave

    Monitors all sensor.*_node_status entities. Fires an alert when a node is reported dead by Z-Wave JS, and a recovery alert when it comes back online. Notification channels and mute periods are configured per-node in the Z-Wave tab.

    Zigbee

    Monitors all sensor.*_last_seen entities created by Zigbee2MQTT. Requires last_seen to be enabled in Z2M and the Last Seen entities to be enabled in HA. Notification channels and mute periods are configured per-device in the Zigbee tab.

    How many hours without a check-in before a device is considered offline. Set this higher than your Z2M availability timeout.
